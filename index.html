Our Story

About Bala Silks

Founded in 1960 by Murugesan Perumal, Bala Silks started as a modest weaving unit with just two handlooms, driven by a passion for fine craftsmanship.

Through dedication and innovation, Bala Silks has grown into one of India's respected silk weaving companies, operating more than 500 handlooms.

Our journey reflects our commitment to preserving traditional silk weaving while embracing modern advancements in craftsmanship and design.

For nearly five decades, Bala Silks supplied premium silk sarees exclusively to wholesale retailers across India. In 2008, recognizing growing customer demand, we entered the retail business — allowing families to experience our premium silk sarees directly from the source.
